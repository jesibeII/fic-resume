How many years have I been writing fic for?

(too many)

I have been writing fic since around 6th grade. I wrote it obsessively and whenever I could. I would write it on my phone or my ipad. Anywhere I could really. One of my clearest memories is of when I was writing fanfiction in a laundromat during a road trip I took with my mom.

I haven't had a steady progression since then as I think I took a bit of a break at the beginning of high school. But at that point I had made the transition from wattpad to fanfiction.net and to archiveofourown.org. Starting once again in 2018, I barely had time to stop.

Currently, my total word count on archiveofourown.org is 797,917 words and is showing no signs of stopping.
